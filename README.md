## About Steal Brainrot

**[Steal Brainrot](https://stealbrainrotgame.io/)** is an addictive **multiplayer io game** that blends fast-paced stealing mechanics with viral internet culture. The player's goal is simple yet chaotic: **steal the most brainrot from opponents** while protecting your own stash in real-time competitive matches.

Featuring quick rounds, unpredictable gameplay, and meme-fueled humor, **Steal Brainrot** combines competitive strategy with modern internet culture. It's quickly becoming a favorite in the **io game genre**, capturing players who love high-energy competition and viral trends.

## Gameplay Overview

### Steal, Defend, and Dominate
- Enter fast-paced matches where every player competes to collect and steal brainrot.
- Use strategic moves to raid opponents' stashes while defending your own collection.
- Watch your score climb as you outsmart rivals in chaotic, action-packed rounds.

### Power-Ups and Strategy
- Unlock special abilities and power-ups that give you temporary advantages.
